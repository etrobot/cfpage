How to Use ChatGPT to Create a Complete TikTok Strategy for Real Estate Agents

Artificial intelligence (AI) has taken the real estate industry by storm. But ultimately, people don’t really know how to properly leverage it.

I know from experience that many real estate agents struggle when it comes to content strategy, especially for TikTok. So in this post, I’ll show you exactly how to use ChatGPT to create a complete TikTok strategy and video blueprint.

Overview

Here’s what we’ll cover:

  • Using ChatGPT to generate 12 video topic ideas for a monthly TikTok strategy
  • Creating 30-second video scripts with ChatGPT
  • Sending the scripts to yourself and importing them into BigVu to turn your phone into a teleprompter
  • Recording videos easily by reading the exact script off your phone screen

Generating Video Topics with ChatGPT

First, I gave ChatGPT some context about my goal of creating educational and lifestyle TikTok videos to attract people to live in Calgary.

Then I asked it to provide 12 video topic ideas that I can turn into 30-second TikTok videos.

ChatGPT generated a great list of ideas:

  • Reasons to move to Calgary
  • Calgary’s vibrant job market
  • The diversity of industries in Calgary beyond oil and gas
  • Joining Calgary’s community of driven professionals
  • Calgary valuing innovation
  • The affordable housing market
  • Calgary’s beautiful natural landscapes
  • Outdoor activities like hiking and skiing
  • Calgary’s arts and culture scene
  • Great restaurants and breweries
  • Calgary’s sunny weather
  • Low taxes in Alberta

Creating Video Scripts with ChatGPT

Next, I picked the first video topic and asked ChatGPT to write a 30-second video script about Calgary’s vibrant job market.

It provided an excellent first draft. I then gave it some feedback to adjust the tone and length. After a few back and forth messages, the final script was ready.

I sent the script to myself by email so I could access it on my phone.

Importing Scripts into BigVu Teleprompter App

I downloaded the BigVu app on my phone which turns your device into a teleprompter. This allows me to read the ChatGPT script right off my phone screen while recording.

In the app, I:

  • Created a new script and pasted the ChatGPT text
  • Adjusted the scroll speed based on my talking pace
  • Hit record and delivered the script while looking at the camera
  • Trimmed extra filler words
  • Added auto-generated captions
  • Downloaded the final video to upload natively to TikTok

Results

Using this AI-powered process, I’m now able to quickly create highly engaging TikTok videos that sound natural and compelling.

The video topics and scripts from ChatGPT give me a huge headstart. And the BigVu teleprompter app makes recording videos a breeze.

I’m now consistent with posting 3 great TikTok videos per week. My views and followers are steadily growing. And I’m starting to convert viewers into real estate leads!

Let me know if you have any other questions. And be sure to try this strategy to step up your real estate TikTok game!
