Top 10 Instagram Algorithm Updates and Leaked Projects for 2023

Instagram is constantly evolving with new features and algorithm updates. As a social media manager, it can be difficult to keep up with all the changes. In this post, we’ll overview the top 10 most important Instagram updates so far in 2023, including some leaked projects that haven’t been officially announced yet.

1. Notes

  • Launched in December 2022
  • Allows users to create status updates that appear in followers’ inboxes
  • Useful for starting conversations and making recommendations

2. Reels Auto Publishing

  • Launched January 2023
  • Lets you schedule Reels to auto-post in the Instagram app
  • Helps creators plan content in advance

3. Quiet Mode

  • Launched January 2023
  • Pick times where you don’t get notifications
  • Instagram batches notifications for when quiet mode ends

4. Multi-Select Not Interested

  • Launched January 2023
  • Long press multiple posts in Explore and select Not Interested
  • Cleans up your Explore feed

5. Gifts to Creators

  • Launched February 2023 in the US
  • Viewers can purchase virtual gifts on Reels to support creators
  • Gifts can be cashed out once creators hit minimum balance

6. Broadcast Channels

  • Launched February 2023
  • One-to-many messaging channels in DMs
  • Creators can share updates, followers can only react

7. Meta Verified

  • Launched February 2023
  • Monthly subscription for verification, increased reach, live chat support
  • Faced backlash, so increased reach was removed in March

8. Reels Trends

  • Launched April 2023
  • Creators can view trending hashtags and sounds in Professional Dashboard
  • Makes it easier to jump on trends

9. Enhanced Reels Features

  • Launched April 2023
  • Improved editing tools allow more customization
  • New analytics like average watch time and total watch time

10. Multiple Links in Bio

  • Launched April 2023
  • Add up to 5 links with descriptions in profile bio
  • Alternative to link in bio tools like Linktree

Leaked Projects

Here are some unreleased features leaked by Alessandro Paluzzi (@alex193a):

  • Barcelona: A Twitter alternative app that syncs with Instagram

  • Collaboration Status: Lets creators indicate they are open to collaborating

  • Voice Memos: Ability to speed up and auto-play voice memos in DMs

Stay tuned for more Instagram updates! Let me know in the comments which changes you’re most excited about.
