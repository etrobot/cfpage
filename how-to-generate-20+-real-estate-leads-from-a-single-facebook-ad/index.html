How to Generate 20+ Real Estate Leads from a Single Facebook Ad

Facebook ads can be an incredibly powerful way to generate leads for your real estate business if done correctly. In this post, I’ll walk you through step-by-step how to create a highly targeted Facebook ad campaign that can result in over 20 closed deals from a single ad.

I recently helped an agent create an ad that generated over 20 buyer and seller leads that led to closed transactions. This ad was different than most real estate Facebook ads in a few key ways:

  • It was optimized for the current housing market conditions (rising interest rates, more buyers looking to downsize).
  • It attracted both buyers AND sellers even though it was positioned for buyers initially.
  • It followed Meta’s latest requirements and policies to avoid having the ad disabled or account banned.

With the right targeting, messaging, creative, and lead capture form, you can replicate the success of this ad for your own real estate business.

Here’s how to create a high-converting Facebook ad step-by-step:

Step 1: Set Up Your Campaign Objectives

  • Go to Facebook Ads Manager and click “+ Create” to make a new campaign.
  • Name your campaign something related to the audience - for example “Downsizing in Calgary June 2023”.
  • Set the Objective to “Lead Generation”.
  • Set the special ad category to “Housing” and select your country.

Step 2: Configure Your Ad Set

  • Name your ad set something related to the audience as well - for example “Calgary Downsizing Buyers and Sellers”.
  • For the conversion event, leave it as “Instant Form” to generate leads.
  • Connect your Facebook business page.
  • Set a daily budget of at least $20 per day.
  • Enter your location targeting - recommended to use 15 mile radius around your city.

Step 3: Create Your Ad

  • Upload an image of an attractive property in your market. Make sure it’s high quality.
  • For the ad copy, focus on messaging around downsizing and customizing a list of homes.
  • Use a headline like “Check out our list of homes that Calgarians are raving about”.
  • Use a call to action of “Learn More” - this converts higher than “Sign Up”.

Step 4: Build Your Lead Capture Form

  • Create an Instant Form and name it something related to the ad - “Downsize Buyers and Sellers”.
  • Go with Higher Intent over More Volume if possible.
  • Ask for Name, Email, Phone Number in order.
  • Add your privacy policy link.
  • Enable review screen so leads confirm info.
  • In messaging, mention accessing a “customizable list of downsized homes”.
  • Link to a filtered MLS search on your website with smaller home options.

Step 5: Review and Launch Your Ad

  • Review ad preview on Facebook, Instagram and Marketplace.
  • Double check targeting and budget.
  • Publish your ad and let it run for at least 2 weeks, making optimization tweaks.

And that’s it! With the right creative and targeting focused on downsizing buyers and sellers, this type of ad can generate multiple high-intent leads for your business.

Make sure to follow all of Meta’s latest policies and requirements around real estate ads. Avoid common mistakes that can get your ad or account disabled. With the right strategy, you can replicate the success of generating 20+ leads from a single Facebook ad.
