How to Break 6 Figures in Your First Year as a Real Estate Agent

Here are the top 10 things to do as a new real estate agent to break 6 figures in your first year:

  1. Create a personal brand and stand out from other agents. Pick a color, style, and niche.

  2. Choose one prospecting method like door knocking and practice scripts daily. This consistency is key.

  3. Post 2 videos per week on YouTube as evergreen content to build authority and demonstrate market knowledge.

  4. Post 2-3 times per week on Instagram, TikTok etc. to build relationships and get people to know the real you.

  5. Use a CRM religiously to organize and nurture leads into clients. One client can turn into 7 with proper follow up.

  6. Host open houses weekly to build your database, get listings, and improve your skills.

  7. Study market data for 30-60 minutes daily to become a true expert regardless of age or experience.

  8. Attend networking events to tap into niches and communities aligned with your passions and hobbies.

  9. Join the right brokerage that provides training, support, culture, tools and resources to help you thrive.

  10. Maintain a well-rounded schedule that incorporates prospecting, content creation, health and fitness. Don’t just grind.

If you consistently apply this proven blueprint daily in your first year, you can break 6 figures as a new real estate agent.
