How to Create Instagram Reels in 2023 - Step-by-Step Tutorial

Instagram Reels continue to be one of the best ways to reach new audiences and grow your following in 2023. In this post, I’ll walk you through how to use the Reels editor in the Instagram app to create amazing Reels from start to finish.

Finding the Reels Editor

When you open the Instagram app, swipe left from your main feed to access the camera and creative tools. At the bottom, tap on “Reels” to open up the Reels editor.

Filming Your Clips

There are a few key features to be aware of when filming your clips for Reels:

  • Audio: Search for and add music, samples or other audio to your Reel. You can also save audio to easily find it again later.

  • Timer: Set a countdown timer which is super handy if you’re trying to lip sync or line up transitions.

  • Camera: Switch between front and back camera. You can also film both at the same time with the new dual camera mode.

Simply tap the large white circle button to start recording a clip. Tap again to stop filming. You can always re-film clips or add in footage from your camera roll too.

Editing Your Reels

Once you’ve filmed all your clips, it’s time to put them together! Here are some key editing features:

  • Edit Clips: Cut clips shorter or longer, rearrange them, delete unused clips.

  • Transitions: Add cool transitions between clips.

  • Text: Easily add text captions and play with font, color and placement.

  • Filters: Add filters to your entire Reel or individual clips.

Make sure to save your Reel drafts to your camera roll as you edit to avoid losing your work!

Adding the Finishing Touches

Before posting your Reel, you can add the cover photo, write a caption, tag products and more. Here are some final tips:

  • Choose an eye-catching cover photo from your camera roll or Reel clips.

  • Write a compelling caption and keywords for discovery.

  • Tag any brands, collaborators or products featured.

  • Turn on “Shared to Feed” so your Reel gets more views.

  • Add captions for accessibility.

And that’s it! You’re ready to share an amazing Instagram Reel. Have fun and get creative with all the features the Reels editor offers. Let me know if you have any other questions!
