How to Start a YouTube Channel as a Real Estate Agent in 2023

YouTube can be an incredibly powerful platform for real estate agents looking to grow their business and attract new clients. In this blog post, I’ll share my top tips on how agents can start their own YouTube channel from scratch and leverage it to generate leads.

Equipment

You don’t need fancy equipment to get started. Your smartphone is the best tool - I still use my iPhone to film most of my videos. For audio, a shotgun mic that attaches to your phone makes it sound more professional. A tripod and some simple lighting like a ring light can also help improve your video quality.

However, don’t forget about natural lighting too. Filming near large windows takes advantage of sunlight and produces great looking footage for free.

Editing

Apps like iMovie or KineMaster make editing easy right on your phone. Eventually, you may want to outsource editing as your channel grows. But many agents edit themselves in the beginning.

Thumbnails

Creating eye-catching thumbnails is key. Use a tool like Canva to design thumbnails that make people want to click.

Content Schedule

Posting consistently, once per week, is important. Consider what types of real estate focused videos you want to produce and schedule times to film, edit and upload them.

Filming

Set aside at least an hour to film, and don’t worry about retakes. Get comfortable on camera, and keep videos 5-7 minutes long. How-to videos and home tours tend to do really well.

Uploading

Write a catchy, keyword-rich title and description to optimize your video to be discovered. Include your contact info so viewers can reach out.

Be Consistent

It takes time to build an audience on YouTube. Stick with it, continuously add new videos, and leads will start coming in. Subscriber numbers aren’t as important as views.

If you stay consistent with creating valuable content, YouTube can be an amazing lead generation machine for your real estate business. Let me know if you have any other questions!
