How to Create 30 Pieces of Content in 24 Hours Using AI

We’ve almost all interacted with AI in some form, whether using it to create content or just consuming AI-based content. This means the bar for content quality has been raised. However, if we utilize AI tools to assist our content creation process, we can not only be successful in our business but also create pieces of work we wouldn’t be capable of before.

Tools Used

  • Nvidia InVideo - Online video editor with integrated AI tools like ChatGPT to generate scripts and AI voiceovers.
  • ChatGPT - Generate video scripts and ideas.
  • AI Voiceovers - Add realistic voiceovers to videos.

The Process

Here’s a quick overview of how to leverage these tools to create lots of content quickly:

  1. Start with an InVideo template to give your video some initial structure and design.

  2. Use ChatGPT integrated in InVideo to generate a script for your video based on prompts you provide. Give ChatGPT detailed prompts for best results.

  3. InVideo will automatically find b-roll footage to match your script.

  4. Use InVideo’s built-in AI voiceover tool to add a voiceover reading your script.

  5. Customize the look by adding your own graphics, logos, text, etc. Search InVideo’s stock media library for relevant clips.

  6. Export your video and then duplicate the project to easily create multiple variations on the same theme.

The key is letting the AI tools do the heavy lifting - writing, finding footage, creating voiceovers. This allows you to create a lot of content quickly. Add your own personal touch with custom graphics and clips. Making multiple variations on similar content helps build consistency and authority.

With these AI-powered tools, it’s possible to create 30+ pieces of vertical video content optimized for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts and more in just 24 hours!
