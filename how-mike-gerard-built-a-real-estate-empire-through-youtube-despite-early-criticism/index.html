How Mike Gerard Built a Real Estate Empire Through Youtube Despite Early Criticism

Mike Gerard is a top real estate agent from Calgary, Canada who has built a massive organization and brand through YouTube, despite facing criticism and self-doubt early on.

In his inspiring presentation, Mike shared how he started creating real estate videos on YouTube five years ago, even though he was insecure, overweight, and inexperienced on camera. His first videos were amateurish - he didn’t look at the camera, had bad audio, and faced ridicule in the comments.

But Mike persisted, steadily improving his content over 500+ videos. He focused on being unbiased and informative to attract an audience that didn’t know him. Mike also featured diverse agents from his organization to appeal to different demographics.

The turning point was a video that led to 252 agents joining his team. Mike credits his success to principles like delayed gratification, consistency, discipline, and trusting the process. Even when facing challenges, he designed “floors” as his minimum effort.

Mike believes anyone can succeed on YouTube by starting with small steps, like 16 minutes a day. Despite early criticism, he kept pushing himself outside his comfort zone to ultimately build a real estate empire and change lives. As Mike says, making your loved ones dreams come true is worth far more than any negative comments.
