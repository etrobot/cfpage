The Ultimate Open House Strategy to 10x Your Real Estate Business

Open houses can be a great way to generate new leads and clients for your real estate business. However, many agents don’t have an effective strategy and end up wasting time at open houses without seeing results.

In this post, we’ll break down an incredible open house strategy used by top producing real estate agent Matthew Nicholas. By implementing his proven techniques, you can start converting every open house into new buyer and seller leads.

Use the “Starbucks Experience” to Create Value

The key is to focus on providing an exceptional experience, similar to what Starbucks delivers. When people feel welcomed and valued, they’ll be more likely to want to do business with you.

Here are some of Matthew’s tips:

  • Greet everyone who comes in, introduce yourself, and shake their hand. This personal connection is huge.

  • Write their name on a folder to give them. This “Starbucks trick” makes people feel recognized.

  • Give them useful neighborhood guides, listing maps, etc. Provide tangible value upfront.

  • Make small talk and get their insights on the home. Build rapport.

  • Qualify them as buyers or sellers based on their answers. Then provide tailored value.

Capture Leads with a VIP Buyers List

Matthew leverages a “VIP buyers list” to convert open house visitors into leads. Here’s how it works:

  • Tell visitors about your exclusive VIP list for off-market home deals.

  • Let them sign up to get exclusive listings before they hit the MLS.

  • People love feeling like VIPs and getting access others don’t have.

  • Capture their name, email, and phone by having them sign up.

  • Offer this to every open house visitor. It works incredibly well to generate leads.

Follow Up and Convert with Automated Emails

Now that you’ve captured leads, you need to convert them into clients with effective follow up.

Matthew uses KB Core and automated emails to nurture his open house leads into transactions.

  • Send a welcome email within 24 hours to engage them.

  • Share off-market listings from Facebook groups with the VIP list.

  • Send valuable market updates and guides over 8 weeks.

  • Use conversational email copy, not robotic templates.

  • Call to action to have them reach out if they need anything.

  • Leverage automated texts at key times too.

Following these open house best practices can 10x your lead generation and sales. Focus on value first, and the results will follow.
